## Workout

### :sweat_drops: Warm-up

+ [ ] **`75 rep`** &emsp;\|&emsp; Jumping Jack
+ [ ] **`75 rep`** &emsp;\|&emsp; Mountain climber
+ [ ] **`75 rep`** &emsp;\|&emsp; Reverse mountain climber
+ [ ] **`03 min`** &emsp;\|&emsp; Skipping

> **Warning**  
> Perform **dynamic stretching** before warm-up\.
